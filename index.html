‚ùå Common Misconceptions

‚úÖ FACT: One drip per second = 11,000+ liters per year. Wastes water, increases bill, moisture causes mold. Fix immediately.
MYTH: "Pour boiling water to clear grease clogs" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Boiling water can crack porcelain fixtures and melt PVC joints. Use hot (not boiling) water with dish soap instead.
MYTH: "Baking soda + vinegar unclogs drains" ‚ùå PARTIALLY WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Good for maintenance/odors, but WON'T clear serious clogs. The fizzing reaction is weak. Use auger for real blockages.

‚úÖ FACT: Harsh chemicals can corrode old pipes, harm septic systems, create toxic fumes. Overuse = pipe damage. Mechanical removal safer.
MYTH: "Lemons/citrus peels freshen garbage disposals" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Acids can corrode metal parts. Use ice cubes + dish soap instead to clean and sharpen blades.
MYTH: "Toilets flush opposite direction in different hemispheres" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Coriolis effect too weak for toilets. Flush direction determined by jet design in bowl, not Earth's rotation.
MYTH: "Running water helps garbage disposal work better" ‚ùå PARTIALLY WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: COLD water is correct (solidifies grease so it can be chopped). Hot water melts grease = clogs pipes downstream.

‚úÖ FACT: Many contain bleach/chemicals that corrode tank parts (rubber flapper, gaskets). Causes leaks. Manual cleaning safer.

‚úÖ FACT: Only human waste and toilet paper designed to dissolve. "Flushable" wipes DON'T dissolve = clogs/damage. Trash bin everything else.
MYTH: "Plumber's putty and silicone are interchangeable" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Putty for REMOVABLE seals (drain baskets). Silicone for PERMANENT waterproof seals (shower edges). Using wrong one = leaks.
MYTH: "Higher water pressure is always better" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: >80 PSI stresses pipes/fixtures = premature failure. Install pressure regulator if too high. Optimal = 50-60 PSI.
MYTH: "Grout seals and waterproofs" ‚ùå WRONG
‚úÖ FACT: Grout is POROUS, water passes through. Waterproofing membrane UNDER tiles is critical. Grout + caulk seal = surface protection only.
MYTH: "PH homes don't need P-traps because it's always hot" ‚ùå DANGEROUS
‚úÖ FACT: P-traps block SEWER GAS (methane, hydrogen sulfide) which is toxic/explosive. Climate irrelevant. Always required by code.

‚úÖ FACT: WD-40 is degreaser/water displacer, not lubricant. Use silicone-based faucet grease. WD-40 temporary only.
